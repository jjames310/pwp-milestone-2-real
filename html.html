Milestone 2

      • My website that is a portfolio, will consist of 4 sections. It will have also have a background image,image of myself, and images of my projects I have done.

      • My first section will be my name and my title and also a brief description of the website
  • About me

  • My projects
      • My third section will consist of a brief overview of some projects I have done with also a picture showing the completed project.
  • Contact Me
      • My fourth and final section will consist of links of professional websites such as Twitter, and Linkedin. It will also provide employers a way to directly message my email.
